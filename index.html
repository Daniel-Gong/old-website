About Me

Hi There! I'm Dongyu Gong. I'm currently an undergraduate of Department of Psychology, Tsinghua University. My research mainly focuses on the mechanisms of attentional capture and singleton suppression. I am interested in how salient items capture our visual attention and how we learn to suppress salient distractors.
